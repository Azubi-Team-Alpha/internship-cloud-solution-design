## Account Setup Steps

<Steps>

1. **Create your AlphaPay account**
   Visit alphapay.africa and sign up with your phone number or email. A one-time passcode (OTP) will be sent for verification.

2. **Complete KYC identity verification**
   Upload a valid government-issued ID (passport, national ID, or driver's licence) and a selfie. Verification completes in under 2 minutes for most users.

3. **Fund your wallet**
   Add money via bank transfer, mobile money (MTN, Telecel, AirtelTigo), or debit/credit card. Your balance is available instantly.

4. **Make your first transfer**
   Enter the recipient's phone number or account number, select the amount, and confirm. Money arrives in under 2 seconds.

</Steps>


## Developer Quickstart

<Aside type="tip">
  Get your API keys from the Developer section of your AlphaPay dashboard. Always use test-mode keys during development.
</Aside>

To initiate a transfer via the API, send a POST request to our transfers endpoint:

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.alphapay.africa/v1/transfers \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "amount": 500,
    "currency": "GHS",
    "recipient_phone": "+233241234567",
    "network": "mtn",
    "description": "Salary payment"
  }'
```
